# Concept Ideas
## Plotline
The main character, [player_name], is your average person. Average school, average grades, average job, average life, average family. 

### Small Details to Include
I want there to be a huge plot twist where it's revealed that the main character's family was crap, or that the ringleader of the circus's family was a pile of dogwater. This would help me reinforce a more dramatic atmosphere, and add an element of underlaying horror---the fact that the danger could be so close. I love exploring those shitty, dramatic family plotlines, just because they usually lead to so many routes. Maybe the main character is convinced that their life is dreary because everyone around them has the same depressed, shitty-ish household. And the main character is just another part of that in the grand scheme of things.

Maybe the main character has this sense of aphantasia like symptoms after they get into the circus and see really cool things because the world is just so brilliantly bright to them that they can't take it. Like, the world is two shades too much, and suddenly they feel alive and loved again ebcause of the presnece of color, but in truth there is a grand lacking absence. Maybe the absence is the lack of love that they have. Also, I think that they should be traumatized and hallucinate a caregiver because they were never really given the proper love and care they deserve while they were still outside of the circus.

The main character should only ever really feel proper affection after entering the circus, and even that should be a bit misguided.

## Settings
The main setting should be in a circus, considering the fact that the main character is someone trying to escape the utter mundaneity of their acerage life. Thus, since circuses always have that super dramatic, playful, even *joyful* atmosphere, I really want to put my pessimist / nihilist / sad main character into this painful little role. 

This setting, IMO, would really foster some character development that is MUCH needed... be it good or bad :eyes:. 

Anyways, so the main character DOES come from a sad dreary town. They sorta feel like they fade from existence, and constantly feel behind from others, as well as lower in terms of personality and love. They aren't really cared for by their parents, who are both very absent. I won't make any of them dead... not yet. Or actually, maybe there is one dead parent, so the main character hallucinates another caretaker to make up for it.

### Color Palettes
Main focus should be a contrasting color palette, with warm and cool tones. The tones in general, however, should be very muted and dulled. The brighter colors are reserved for use in impact frames, dramatic scenes, or just scenes that require extreme lighting that emphasizes the horror or psychological aspects of the game. 

The circus itself should employ super vibrant shades, like bright reds, shimmering golds, and all of the bright lights that would typically blind you when it's your first time in a circus.
